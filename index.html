Welcome to the Memory of Kerotscraft

This wasn’t just a Minecraft server. It was a place where strangers became friends, where walls weren’t just made of cobblestone they were built with trust, laughter, and late-night adventures.

From survival builds to unexpected creeper explosions, every player left their mark. We built homes, farms, memories and a community that felt like family.

The server is gone now, retired so its creator could chase what truly matters. But for those of us who were there, those memories still live on in screenshots, in stories, and in here.
